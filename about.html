My name is Lieske Keegstra, I am an animator and illustrator from the Netherlands. I love creating work that moves me to delve deeper into my faith and learn more about God and His love for us. Alongside this, I create artworks for children, which includes commissions such as handmade paintings for kids bedrooms. My animation work is varied in styles and materials, from stop motion paper cut-out animation to 2D animated posters, focused on incorporating traditional techniques.
